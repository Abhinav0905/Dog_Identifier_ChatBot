# Corporate Social Responsibility
Source: https://dharamsalaanimalrescue.org/be-a-corporate-partner

Corporations and Foundations partner with Dharamsala Animal Rescue to support our efforts to create a healthier and safer community for street dogs and people. By funding our Animal Birth Control, Anti Rabies, Rescue, and Rural Humane Education programs, together we can end the human/street dog conflict.

Our 2025 needs include; a veterinary biochemistry analyser, a new jeep for catching and rescues, funding for our November 2025 sterilisation camp, land purchase or long term rental to build out a bigger shelter/clinic, ongoing medical supplies, pet food, and more. Please get in touch if you are interested to learn more.

### We align our work with the United Nations’ Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)—the global blueprint for a better future—making DAR a strong partner for your Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives.

SDG 3: Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages Through lifesaving vaccination, humane dog population management, and disease prevention programs—including our recognized role as a Rabies Center for Excellence —we improve community health and safeguard lives.

SDG 11: Make cities and human settlements inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable With our humane dog population management (ABC) and rural outreach education programs, we are reducing the number of strays and the number of dog bites, making communities safer for both people and animals.

SDG 8: Promote sustained, inclusive and sustainable economic growth, full and productive employment and decent work for all DAR employs and trains local residents to run our programs. We also support the local economy—from medical suppliers to subsistence farmers—creating ripple effects that benefit the broader community.

## Current CSR Partner Projects

## Exusia Sponsored our March 2025 Sterilisation Camp

We thank Exusia for their CSR grant funding 50% of our sterilisation camp from March 5 – 21, 2025 allowing us to sterilise and vaccinate 307 dogs. You can learn more about our Animal Birth Control program here.

## Punjab Bevel Gears LTD Sponsored our November 2024 Sterilisation Camp

We thank Punjab Bevel Gears Limited for sponsoring our November 2024 sterilisation camp where we sterilised and vaccinated 407 dogs. Sterilisation is the best way to improve the health of street dogs, stabilise population growth, stop unnecessary suffering of unwanted puppies, and prevent zoonotic diseases, like rabies, from spreading,

## SML Films Limited Gives Monthly for Ongoing Shelter Expenses

By giving monthly to support our work, SML Films Limited provides reliable support that allows DAR to plan ahead, respond to emergencies faster, and sustain long-term programs.

## Acana Donates Pet Food

Donated goods reduce our monthly costs and provide an opportunity for brand awareness. Our staff and patients will promotes your brand across our social media platforms getting your product in front of tens of thousands of dog-loving, compassionate consumers. Thank you Acana.
