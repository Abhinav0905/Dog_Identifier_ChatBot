# Double Your Donation
Source: https://dharamsalaanimalrescue.org/does-your-company-match-donations

Did you know that can double your donation to Dharamsala Animal Rescue through our matching donation program?

Many employers sponsor matching gift programs to match charitable contributions made by their employers. If you volunteer with us, your company may also offer a volunteer grant program.

Simply type your employer’s name in the search bar below to find out if they will match your gift to DAR!
