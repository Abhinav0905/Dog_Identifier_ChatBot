# Introducing our Dharma Dog Shop
Source: https://dharamsalaanimalrescue.org/dharma-dog-shop

If you didn’t know, Dharamsala is the home of the Dalai Lama , and India is the birthplace of yoga. So, it makes sense for our meditating dog design to be the focus of our product line.

We have tees, sweatshirts, hoodies, mugs and new phone cases! Seeing the great support for our Dharma Dog line, we added additional lines like our popular “You Can’t Buy Love But you Can Rescue it” motto, or our new one “I Love a Rescue Dog”.

All profits go toward helping end the human/street dog conflict in Dharamsala, India, by funding our programs : spay/neuter, rabies vaccination, rescue and adoption, and community education for rabies safety and compassion.

By wearing a Dharma Dog item, you are also creating awareness of our work and we cannot thank you enough for that!

#### So Be Calm and Help Dogs, because shopping here will not make you feel guilty one bit!
