# Adopt Rosa - Dharamsala Animal Rescue
Source: https://dharamsalaanimalrescue.org/adopt-rosa

### If you live in the Kangra District of H.P. and are interested in adopting Rosa, please call 98828 58631 Monday – Saturday 9:30am – 5pm to set up an appointment to meet her and fill out an application.

If you are in the US or in Canada and want to adopt, please click the International Adoption button below.

We instantly fell in love with her – a beautiful black and white puppy. Rosa is literally the happiest girl we’ve seen. In the short time we’ve known her, we can tell she’s incredibly resilient and brave. She’s left us all shocked with how quickly she recovered from her fracture and has adapted to moving around on her three little legs. She didn’t even need time to adjust she just knew exactly what to do! It was incredible to witness her victory run around the shelter once she healed.

If you’ve always wanted a super hero dog with an origin story, she’s the girl for you! Funny, playful, adorably cute with no clue that she’s missing something, Rosa will make the perfect pet for someone who wants a dog with tons of heart. Trust us when we say, this girl can do anything a 4 legged dog can do.

P.S.: she runs so fast when we shake the treat bag
