# Career Opportunities | Dharamsala Rescue
Source: https://dharamsalaanimalrescue.org/career-opportunities

### If you are interested in applying for these opportunities, please send resume and cover letter to email address included in the posting.

### Please put the position title you are applying for in the “subject” line of the email. Thank you for wanting to work with Dharamsala Animal Rescue.

### We look forward to hearing from you.

We’re looking for passionate, purpose-driven leaders based in the USA to join Dharamsala Animal Rescue’s Board of Directors and help us end the Human/Street Dog Conflict in India.

This is more than a board seat. It’s an opportunity to be part of a movement that protects both communities and animals, educates children, and builds a more compassionate world.

We’re especially seeking individuals who bring:

- Financial Investment & Fundraising – You believe in this mission enough to invest in it and help us grow our donor community.
- Accounting, Finance & Budget Expertise – You can help us steward our resources wisely and build a sustainable organization.
- A Heart for International Impact – You want your leadership to ripple far beyond your own backyard.
Whether you’re a seasoned nonprofit board member or stepping into your first board role with the right skills and passion, we’d love to hear from you.

What you’ll gain: A meaningful leadership role in a growing nonprofit. Connection with a global community of changemakers. The knowledge that your time and investment is saving lives. Ready to make a difference? Fill out our application here: https://lnkd.in/g2SSzz_Q
