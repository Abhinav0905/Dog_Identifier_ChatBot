# Adopt Molly - Dharamsala Animal Rescue
Source: https://dharamsalaanimalrescue.org/adopt-molly

### If you live in the Kangra District of H.P. and are interested in adopting Molly, please call 98828 58631 Monday – Saturday 9:30am – 5pm to set up an appointment to meet her and fill out an application.

If you are in Canada or in the US and want to adopt, please click the International Adoption button below.

Shy as she is, little Molly is a real brave-heart who’s getting stronger every day. Honestly you haven’t lived till you’ve seen her trotting around on her little legs with those velvet brown ears flapping in the wind. Two of her favorite things are: playing with other puppies at the shelter and snuggling into hooman’s lap. A real heartbreaker in the making. When she first came to us hurt and crying, we couldn’t imagine she would make such a brilliant recovery! The best part though is that she didn’t have to lose her leg to a cruel incident of hit and run. God bless Molly!

Please reach out because Molly is eager to hear from you. She’s going to make the perfect soulmate for someone really special. This adoption is for permanent Dharamsala residents only.
