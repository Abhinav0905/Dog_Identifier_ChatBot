# Become a Street Dog Hero
Source: https://dharamsalaanimalrescue.org/donate-monthly2

In our small town of Dharamsala, we are doing our part to end the street dog/human conflict though our sterilization, vaccination, rescue, street animal feeding, education, and adoption programs. And YOU can help make a difference by joining our Street Dog Hero Community.

#### Only you can stop the suffering

When you become a Street Dog Hero , you can help provide shelter, food and love, and you can give hundreds of animals a second chance at life. Regular monthly gifts provide a consistent, reliable income stream that allows us to spend less time fundraising—and more time saving lives. Becoming a DAR Street Dog Hero is an easy, efficient and tax-deductible way to make a difference for animals all year long.

#### Only you can prevent more deaths

Your ongoing contributions enable us to sustain our programs over time, helping to ensure that we are serving the street dogs and people of Dharamsala. Please consider a monthly gift today.
