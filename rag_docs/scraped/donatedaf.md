## Dharamsala Animal Rescue’s mission is to create a better world for animals and people by ending the human/street dog conflict.

What causes the conflict?

- India has more stray dogs than any country in the world, an estimated 60 million.
- 1 person dies of rabies every 30 mintues (95% of the time from a dog bite.)
- The government has no national sterilization, vaccination, or education programs to humanely stabilize the population, eradicate rabies, or teach bite safety and compassion.
What does DAR do to decrease the conflict? We provide several key programs to achieve our goals: spay/neuter , rabies vaccination , rescue and rehabilitation , and community education for rabies safety and compassion .
