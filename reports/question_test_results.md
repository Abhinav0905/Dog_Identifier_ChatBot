## 10. What are signs of rabies in dogs?

**Status:** `200`

**Response:**

**Possible rabies signs in dogs:**

- Sudden behaviour change, such as friendly to aggressive or unusually quiet.
- Excessive drooling or foaming at the mouth.
- Trouble swallowing, staggering, disorientation, or weakness.
- Unprovoked aggression or later-stage paralysis.

Do not approach a dog showing these signs. Report the exact location and keep people away.
